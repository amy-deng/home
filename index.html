Research Experience

2017-
Evolutionary Neural Network Algorithm Based on Triplet Nucleotide Coding
Researching on new evolutionary heuristics methods inspired by the mechanism of biological DNA genetic information and evolution, which uses a triplet nucleotide coding scheme to encode the topology and weights of the neural network and a set of new genetic operators to search for globally optimal solutions. Evolved neural networks applied to resolve complex control problems, such as pole balancing and the double pole balancing without velocity problem, a non-Markovian task.
2017-
Autonomous Cognitive Decision System for Multiple Uncertain Information
Designing spike-based neuron and network training model, and a spiking neural network(SNN) training approach based on spike timing dependent plasticity(STDP) to achieve static and dynamic object recognition, such as the MNIST dataset and Small ball moving on the board.
2017
Multi-Layer Neural Network Based on Drosophila Compound Eye
By abstracting the pattern of processing data from the Drosophila visual network, proposed a five-layer network for simulating the motion handling of Drosophila vision, to obtain the distance, direction and the speed of the moving object.
2016-2017
Exploration of a mechanism to form bionic, self-growing and self-organizing neural network
We try to build a bionic method to form self-growing and self-organizing neural networks. We have presented a method for connection growing, verification and optimization. Paralleled way to simulate this process has been applied by using CPU+GPU. A simulation platform and experiments are conducted to verify and test proposed methods, and results proved the method could generate neural networks that are helpful in detecting moving targets.
2017
Face Detection Using Multi-Task Convolutional Neural Networks
Three cascaded convolutional neural networks(CNN) were constructed to predict face and landmark location in a coarse-to-fine manner based on TensorFlow. Preprocessed Wider Face dataset to positive and negative samples by applying OpenCV. The training model has good accuracy in the FDDB data set.
2016
Smart Movie Player with Face Recognition and Information Acquisition
We accomplished a movie player with a protagonist face recognition function which can match with local information in time. Graphics programming implemented by exploiting OpenCV, SDL, and FFmpeg and the face recognition realized by utilizing SeetaFace Engine.
